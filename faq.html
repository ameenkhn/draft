An orthopaedic and spine specialist treats disorders of bones, joints, muscles, ligaments, and the spine. This includes fractures, trauma, back and neck pain, disc problems, joint diseases, deformities, sports injuries, and age-related musculoskeletal conditions.

You should consult an orthopaedic doctor for persistent joint or back pain, fractures, injuries, difficulty in movement, spine-related symptoms, limb deformities, or when conservative treatment has not provided relief.
